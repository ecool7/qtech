Design Model for Product Development

Complete product lifecycle management from concept to production

Quxiang can take responsibility for the development of your technology product throughout the product life cycle, from the initial product idea to the finished product, and on to production management after the development process is complete.

Idea Development

At the stage of idea development, Quxiang clarifies whether your company's plans concerning technology product development matches Quxiang's competencies and resources.

Concept Development

After the concept is approved, Quxiang develops the specifications for the development project and agrees to your company's final terms regarding savings and deadlines.

Development Phase

During development, Quxiang completes the project, documentation, prototypes, and all testing and approval of your new technology product.

Production Management

After development, Quxiang launches pilot production and completes the new product introduction (NPI). We can also manage mass production if needed.

Dedicated Project Manager

When you choose Quxiang as your company's new product technology development partner, you get one project manager committed to your development project. The project manager is responsible for the progress and continuity of the project throughout the development process.
