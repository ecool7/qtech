About Quxiang

We build real‑world solutions

Quxiang Technology Limited is an engineering team focused on practical products for everyday life. We combine electronics, sensors, connectivity and software to reduce energy costs, improve comfort and increase safety for homes, small businesses and municipalities.

What we do

  • Green Energy: solar PV design, energy storage, grid integration and monitoring.
  • Heating & HVAC: heat‑pump control, smart thermostats, boiler room automation.
  • Smart City: EV charging, street lighting, telemetry, device management platforms.
  • Smart Home: lighting, climate and security with Matter/Thread/Voice support.

We take responsibility for the entire lifecycle: concept, prototypes, pilots, production ramp‑up and maintenance hand‑over. Customers receive complete documentation and IP rights for the finished product.
